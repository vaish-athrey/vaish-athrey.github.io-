One stop platform for NGOs, student organizations, volunteers and donors

The dynamic team at SocieTree works tirelessly to build a community of like minded people in order to make this world sustainable and bearable for all species.
 We are a team of young college going students who strongly believe that we can and must do things differently, yet efficiently. Our aim is to bring easy access, knowledge and updates about various events taking place at an NGO to all volunteers and donors.

OUR VISION

SocieTree aims to establish an interactive community of various organisations, initiatives, student communities and volunteers by providing a common platform to maximize the reach of noble services.

Our aim is to connect volunteers to non-profits through skill-based volunteering positions and match different organisations with similar interests to conduct collaborative events at a larger scale.
