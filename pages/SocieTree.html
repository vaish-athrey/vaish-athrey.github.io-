ABOUT SOCIETREE

We at SocieTree, are trying to form a network of NGOs which will then facilitate in building a one stop platform where the NGOs, volunteers and donors can all interact. NGOs can market and advertise their goals and events, the volunteers willing to contribute to the welfare of the society via these NGOs can stay informed about the various events being held and interested donors can help the said NGOs by providing them financial aid.
